Best Music Ever

Brandon Aviles brings you the playlist that we'll be listening to and talking about for years. As enigmatic as its author, these albums 'Blonde' by Frank Ocean, 'Based on a Feeling' by Sabrina Claudio, and 'Souled Out' by Jhené Aiko is a daring set of ambient post and present R&B that slips into your bloodstream. Through its rich story-telling, these once in a lifetime artist have created luxorious word-play and melody that these artists favor. From mood-altering moves of 'Nights' by Frank Ocean, to (literally) being transported to the ocean with Jhené Aiko's coastal beach vibes of 'Spotless Mind'; and finally, to your bedrooms relaxtion with Sabrina Claudio' 'Don't Make Me Wait'. These albums will definitely keep you wanting more.
